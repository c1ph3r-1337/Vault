> #Whales - Whales are the Large holders of an asset - (Commonly in crypto or small small-cap equities) can materially move markets due to liquidity asymmerty.

> Supply Shock (Order Book Impact)
- When whales sell large positions:
	- They consume buy-side liquidity across multiple price levels
	- This creates **slippage** (price drops faster than expected)
	- If executed aggressively (market sells), it can cascade through the order book
- Result: **Short-term price dislocation downward**
